## ðŸ“… For Librarians

### Managing Shifts
Go to the **Schedule** tab.
- **Create Shifts**: Define shift periods for each day.
- **Assign Volunteers**: Click on a shift to assign a volunteer.
- **View Coverage**: Quickly see which times are covered and which are empty.

### Managing Tasks
Go to the **Tasks** tab.
- **Create Tasks**: Add new tasks with descriptions, priorities, and due dates.
- **Assign Tasks**: Assign tasks to specific volunteers.
- **Track Progress**: See which tasks are Pending, In Progress, or Completed.

### Tracking Hours
Go to the **Logs** or **Volunteers** tab.
- View detailed history of volunteer check-ins and check-outs.
- See total hours contributed by each volunteer.
- Export reports if needed (via the database tools).

### Announcements
Use the **Announcements** feature to post messages to all volunteers. This is great for:
- Schedule changes
- New policies
- General reminders

## ðŸ™‹ For Volunteers

### Checking In/Out
When you arrive for your shift:
1.  Log in to the dashboard.
2.  Click the **Check In** button.
3.  When leaving, click **Check Out**.
*This automatically tracks your volunteer hours.*

### Viewing Your Schedule
Check the **My Schedule** tab to see when you are assigned to work next.

### Managing Tasks
Check the **My Tasks** tab.
- View tasks assigned to you.
- Mark them as **Completed** when you finish them.
- Report issues if you cannot complete a task.
